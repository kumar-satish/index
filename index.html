Research

My research focuses on dynamic service composition in multi-tenant SaaS cloud. Particularly, I am intereted in analysing "Technical Debt" in service execution environment. This approach provides an economic-driven strategic decision for optimising the service execution environment and maintaining overall service quality in SaaS cloud.

    publication

  • [ICWS 2019] S.Kumar, R. Bahsoon, T.Chen and R. Buyya. Identifying and Estimating Technical Debt for Service Composition in SaaS Cloud, In the Proceeding of 24th IEEE International Conference on Web Services, Italy, 2019.
